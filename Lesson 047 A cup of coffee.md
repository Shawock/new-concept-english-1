## Lesson 47 A cup of coffee

- Do you like coffee, Ann?
- Yes, I do.
- Do you want a cup?
- Yes, please, Christin.
- Do you want any sugar?
- Yes, please.
- Do you want any milk?
- No, thank you. I don't like milk in my coffee. I like `black coffee`.
- Do you like `biscuits`?
- Yes, I do.
- Do you want one?
- Yes, please.

---

> - `black coffee` 黑咖啡（清咖啡; 不加牛奶的咖啡）
> - `biscuit` 小点心，饼干
